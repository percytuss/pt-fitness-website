Empowering Refugee Youth Through Boxing

Learn More

WHAT?

pt² fitness is a place where young people from refugee backgrounds can feel a sense of belonging.

A place where they can rewrite their stories, learn how to box, grow, and discover their strength.

WHO?

Young refugees aged 16 to 25 of all abilities who are in need of a supportive community and a boost in confidence.

WHY?

To build a community where refugees and underserved groups can use boxing to grow, build resilience

and unlock potential through accessible and empowering fitness training rooted in personal growth, community, and respect.
