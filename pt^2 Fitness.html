What We Do

pt² Fitness is more than just boxing. A place where young refugees can feel they belong, rewrite their stories, and grow stronger—inside and out. Here, they discover the art of boxing and the power of their own potential.

Who We Serve

We empower young refugees, aged 16 to 25, of all abilities. They’re individuals looking for confidence, community, and a chance to thrive.

Why It Matters

Our mission is to create a vibrant, inclusive community where boxing becomes a vehicle for transformation. Through accessible, empowering boxing programs, we help young people:

  • Build resilience.
  • Find their inner strength.
  • Unlock their potential.

Rooted in personal growth, respect, and connection, pt² Fitness is about more than training—it’s about changing lives.
