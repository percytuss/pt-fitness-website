Empowering Refugee Youth Through Boxing

What WE DO

pt² fitness is more than just boxing.

A place where young refugees can feel a sense of belonging, can rewrite their stories,

Here, they discover the art of boxing and the power of their potential.

WHO WE SERVE

We empower refugee youth aged 16 to 25 of all abilities.

Individuals looking for confidence, community, and a chance to thrive.

WHY It Matters

Our mission is to create a vibrant, inclusive community where boxing becomes a vehicle for transformation.

Through accessible, empowering boxing programs, we help young people:

  • Build resilience.
  • Find their inner strength.
  • Unlock their potential.

Rooted in personal growth, respect, and connection, pt² Fitness is about more than training—it’s about changing lives.
